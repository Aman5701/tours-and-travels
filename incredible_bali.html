Day Wise Itinerary


DAY 1

  • Hello Bali!

  • Welcome to Bali. Upon your arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport, meet our representative at the arrival hall who will escort you till your private car.

  • Arrive at the hotel and check in. Kuta is where all the action takes place in Bali. Home to a plethora of nightclubs, street shops and the most popular beaches.

  • Visit the Discovery Mall and bring back truckloads of bling for yourself! The beauty of this mall lies not only in shopping but also in its scenic location.

  • As the sun sets you must checkout the famous nightlife spots such as the Rock Bar at Ayana Resort, perched on cliffs above the Indian Ocean.

  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 2

  • Uluwatu Sunset Tour with Kecak Dance

  • Wake up to a new day and refuel your energy with a hearty breakfast meal and head to Tanah Lot Temple, probably the most popular and photographed temple on the island.

  • Tanah Lot Temple stands for Earth (Tanah) and Sea (Lot), quite appropriate considering its spectacular setting on top of an impressive rock overlooking the Indian Ocean. Be mesmerized by the captivating traditional Balinese art performance, which also goes by, ‘the monkey chant dance’, and loosely ‘fire dance’ for its occasional use of fire as a center piece prop.

  • The Kecak dance is unique in that it has no other musical background or accompaniment besides the chanting of male dancers, intoning a “keh-chack” polyrhythmic choir during most of the performance. Kecak’s storyline is taken from the Ramayana Hindu epic and it’s often on the entertainment schedule of almost every Balinese arts and culture venue.

  • At the entrance lane to Tanah Lot Temple, you will find a beautiful architectural Balinese gate and many art shops offering local handicrafts besides paintings, woodcarvings, and more.

  • Overnight stay at your hotel.

DAY 3

  • Water Sports at Benoa Beach

  • Kickstart your morning with the scrumptious breakfast.

  • Add an adrenaline kick to your Bali vacation with a choice of thrilling water sports and activities at Tanjung Benoa beach. Located in the Benoa peninsula far away from the hustle & bustle enjoy a fun banana boat ride, zip along the beach on a jet ski or try exhilarating water sports like parasailing.

  • Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

DAY 4

  • Head to Nusa Dua

  • After breakfast, transfer to hotel in Nusa Dua which is quiet and secluded compared to its neighboring beaches. Check in and spend the rest of the day at leisure.

DAY 5

  • Explore Nusa Penida

  • Kickstart your morning with the scrumptious breakfast.

  • Embark on a Nusa Penida day trip, the largest of the three Nusa islands that are located southeast of Bali across the Badung Strait and enjoy a full day of adventure and breathtaking views. Revel in the joys of its tranquil atmosphere and picturesque landscape. Drop by magnificent photo stops worth posting on your Instagram, such as Angela Billabong, Broken Beach, Klingking Beach, and the Crystal Bay. You'll get the opportunity to swim in the area's crystal-clear waters.

  • You can even go snorkeling in Crystal Bay, Manta Bay, or Wall Bay and discover the majestic marine life in the area. It's a must for any beachgoer visiting Bali.

  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

DAY 6

  • Departure

  • Wake up in the morning, have a scrumptious breakfast and pack your bags for your departure.

  • Complete the check-out formalities and get ready to leave to the airport to catch a suitable flight to back home.

  • Reach your desired destination and end the trip with unforgettable memories.
